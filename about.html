ABOUT US

Your Trusted and reliable Agrovet, Consultancy, Fumigation, Industrial cleaning and Clinical service provider company

OUR HISTORY

Crown Agro-Vet Limited, a leading pest control and management facility, registered and licensed with the Corporate Affairs Commission with a mandate of ensuring the safety of our environment, prevention of pest induced diseases on humans, 3 animals, and livestock, and eradication of economic losses orchestrated by the destruction of our infrastructures by pests and rodents. Added to our expertise on pest control, we provide clinical services to animals and pets, deal in the sales of Agricultural equipment and provide bespoke Agric Consultancy Services. Our team comprises of highly motivated and dedicated professionals whose goal is to ensure we live in a healthy environment. We adhere to environmental health and safety compliance and ensure our clients and customers gets value for their investment

OUR VISION

To be the go-to pest control and agricultural solutions provider, recognized for our commitment to excellence, environmental sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

OUR MISSION

To create safe, healthy, and pest-free environments using sustainable and effective pest control methodologies form both human and animal.
