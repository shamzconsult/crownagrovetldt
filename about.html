ABOUT US

Your Trusted and reliable Agrovet, Consultancy, Fumigation, Industrial cleaning and Clinical service provider company

OUR HISTORY

Crown Agro-Vet Limited, a leading pest control and management facility, registered and licensed with the Corporate Affairs Commission with a mandate of ensuring the safety of our environment, prevention of pest induced diseases on humans, 3 animals, and livestock, and eradication of economic losses orchestrated by the destruction of our infrastructures by pests and rodents. We provide these services to an array of our clients using Integrated Pest Management Strategies that combine the utilization of biological, physical, and chemical pest control methods to prevent the spread of pests and reduce reliance on harmful pesticides. IPM helps control pests while minimizing environmental impact and reducing the development of resistance to pesticides. We utilize eco-friendly methodologies in fumigating homes, offices, schools, and infrastructural facilities, these include the use of natural predators to tackle specific insects, botanical pesticides, and Integrated Pest Management practices to meet the needs of our clients. We utilize a multi-dimensional approach to carrying out our fumigation process amongst which are:

  • Inspection: We examine vulnerability areas, pest entry points, and pest population levels at our client site, determining specific types of pests and developing an approach to tackling them.
  • Preparation: We seal up every entry point for insects and pests and remove cover items and sensitive materials to disallow fumigants from entering them.
  • Fumigation Process: Between 24 and 72 hours, we apply fumigants and ensure all areas are enclosed to prevent chemical splashes and contamination of other non-designated areas of the facility.
  • Aeration and Ventilation: We ensure the facility is aerated and ventilated to remove fumigants. We ensure all areas are open for several hours to remove fumigants from the facility.
  • Post Fumigation Cleaning: We ensure the facility fumigated is cleaned, debris removed and all exposed areas are washed properly. We reassess the area and ensure dead pests are removed and damaged components are fixed.
  • Post-Fumigation Inspection: We ensure that the pest problem has been addressed. In schools and offices. We provide a report on the success of the operation.
