Frequently Asked Questions

What is fumigation, and why is it necessary?

Fumigation is a pest control process that involves using chemicals (fumigants) to eliminate pests such as insects, rodents, and termites. It is essential for protecting your health, infrastructure, and agricultural produce from damage and diseases caused by pests

How often should fumigation be carried out?

The frequency depends on the type of facility and the pest infestation level. For homes, quarterly fumigation is recommended, while commercial spaces may require monthly or bi-monthly treatments.

What types of pests do you handle?

We manage a wide range of pests, including cockroaches, termites, ants, mosquitoes, rodents (rats and mice), and other harmful insects that threaten health and property.
