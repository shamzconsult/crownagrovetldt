OUR SERVICES

At Crown Agrovet Limited, we specialize in protecting people, animal, infrastructure, and agricultural investments from the devastating effects of pests. From reducing the spread of diseases caused by cockroaches, mosquitoes, and rodents to mitigating agricultural losses due to termites and other destructive insects, we are committed to making homes, businesses, and farms safer. Our expertise extends beyond pest control to include: Veterinary services for livestock health, Sale of high-quality agricultural products and equipment, Agricultural consultancy services, With a team of seasoned veterinary doctors and environmental health consultants, we employ the latest technologies and best practices to deliver services that meet and exceed our clients' expectations.

OUR COMPETENCIES

We have committed twenty-seven (27) years of our corporate existence to providing excellent services to an array of clientele. From establishing a vet clinic that takes care of animal needs to providing fumigation services, selling agricultural products, providing agricultural consulting services, and selling agricultural equipment, we have traversed the agricultural and public health ecosystem to provide essential services that put our clients in a safe space to operate without any distraction. Our clients range from public institutions to various private enterprises and non-governmental organizations in Nigeria. We are in constant touch with best practices and ensure our staff are upskilled regularly to meet the demands of our clients. We have in our team, a team of veterinary doctors and environmental health and safety consultants whose experience and expertise cut across pest control and management. Our collective expertise and skills come in handy in satisfying our client's needs. Our experience has endeared us to be registered members of the Pest Control Association of Nigeria, making our organization a one-stop shop for all fumigation services in Abuja. We are certified by the Abuja Municipal Area Council (AMAC), therefore, making our fumigation certificate of completion a valid document that can be tendered to health regulatory agencies.
